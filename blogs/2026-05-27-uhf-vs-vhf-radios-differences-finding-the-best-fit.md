When choosing a two-way radio system, one of the most important decisions is whether to use Ultra High Frequency (UHF) or Very High Frequency (VHF) radios. Understanding the difference between UHF and VHF radios can help organizations improve communication reliability, coverage and worker safety across different environments. In this blog, we’ll compare UHF and VHF radios, explain the pros and cons of each and help you determine which option best suits your operational needs.
